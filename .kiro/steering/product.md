# Product Overview

## SoftVentas - Point of Sale System

SoftVentas is a comprehensive web-based Point of Sale (POS) and inventory management system designed for retail businesses with multi-branch support.

## Core Features

- **Multi-branch Management**: Support for multiple sucursales (branches) with independent configurations
- **User Role System**: Five access levels - Administrador General, Administrador Sucursal, Secretaria, Cajero, Vendedor
- **Inventory Management**: Products, combos, stock control with min/med/max alerts, expiration tracking
- **Sales & Purchases**: Complete transaction flow with invoices, tickets, credit notes
- **Credit Management**: Customer and supplier credit tracking with payment schedules
- **Cash Register**: Daily cash opening/closing (arqueo de caja) with movement tracking
- **Composite Products**: Parent-child product relationships for different presentations (e.g., units vs boxes)
- **Reporting**: PDF and Excel exports for sales, inventory, credits, and financial reports

## Business Domain (Spanish)

The system uses Spanish terminology throughout:
- Sucursal = Branch
- Venta = Sale
- Compra = Purchase
- Producto = Product
- Cliente = Customer
- Proveedor = Supplier
- Factura = Invoice
- Arqueo = Cash register reconciliation
- Traspaso = Stock transfer between branches
- Kardex = Inventory movement log

## Target Users

Small to medium retail businesses in Latin America requiring:
- Multi-location inventory control
- Credit sales management
- Detailed financial reporting
- Role-based access control
